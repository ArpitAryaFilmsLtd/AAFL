Yamuna's Sewage Treatment Plant | Documentary Trailer | Exposing Delhi's Real Picture

As the government keeps on spending crores on Clean Yamuna Project, the main and real problem lies unnoticed in untreated water from sewage treatment plant under the filth. We visit and capture the untreated water from STPs mixing with Yamuna. We take interviews of various environmental activists and talk about the current condition of Yamuna. Why is it like what it is? What is the government doing for years and still not able to make the necessary changes and impact.
